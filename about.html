I'm Tom Harrison, an artist and maker based in the UK


I used to want to fly aeroplanes and then build rockets. I do neither of those things. But what I am

doing is exploring those interests, and my engineering and product design education, within the

framework of a BA Fine Art degree. I create massive sculptures and installations in an effort to try

and inspire that same sense of wonder and fascination of the mechanical, that I have always held

myself. I hope you find yourself captivated; there's a whole universe out there to discover


This website is coded by myself using HTML and CSS. Expect quirks and for the best experience, view

on a computer rather than on a phone screen. If you want to help support my work then please visit

my shop, follow me on social media and check back here for updates on new work / exhibitions


Thanks for visiting - Tom
